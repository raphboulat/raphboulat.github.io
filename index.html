Bio

Ciao! I am a first-year PhD student in Economics at Bocconi University and LEAP affiliate. I am an applied microeconomist focusing on the criminal justice system and racial disparities. I am also interested in applied econometrics methods.

Before my PhD, I worked as a pre-doctoral fellow at Stanford University with Professor Lukas Althoff. I hold an MSc in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics from LSE, an MSc in Economics and a BA in International Relations from UNIGE.
